# Chat 3 - Why learn markup languages?

The purpose of this chat is build research skills related to your career.

  1. After going through the content in markup-languages, find an article, blog post, or video that meets these criteria:

    1. Created by a professional in your desired career field
    2. Related to how one of the concepts in markup-languages is used or not used in your desired career field

  2. Join the the #chat-3 Slack channel

  3. Post the URL of the article you found.

  4. Share your desired career field and 50-100 words on why you think you will or won't leverage the skills discussed in the article in your career
